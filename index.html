Ophelia is a Discord social companion based on GPT 3.5 Turbo

Personalities

Ophelia can switch between multiple personalities as the user desires; changing from a snarky partner to a serious ChatGPT-like assistant or even a playful companion girl.

Pay to Play

Ophelia operates on tokens. To prolong your chats, you can purchase tokens as you go, only paying for what you need.

Memories

Unlike other bots, Ophelia maintains her memories across multiple conversations. It does not matter when or where you left the chat, pick up from anywhere!

Frequently asked questions

What are tokens?

Tokens are virtual currency that you can purchase from us. 100 tokens allow you to send approximately 1 message to Ophelia. By default you are given 1000 tokens to use. You can check your token balance at any time using the command '/account' on Discord.

How do I use Ophelia?

You can add Ophelia as a bot to your own server or join our official Discord server linked below to try out Ophelia. Use '/register' to automatically link up using your Discord account. Use '/talk' to interact with Ophelia.

Are there any privacy features?

You can set your message visibility to private or enable DM mode for free by using the '/visibility' & '/dms' commands on Discord.
